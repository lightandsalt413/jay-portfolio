The Challenge

Growing a salon's digital presence in a competitive market

Janerockstar Salon is a Filipino-American owned beauty salon in Artesia, California specializing in organic hair color, scalp restoration, and hair botox treatments. The challenge was building a strong social media presence that showcased their unique expertise while attracting new clients and building trust in a competitive local market.

The Solution

Consistent content that builds brand trust

I manage the salon's daily social media content — creating before-and-after transformation posts, educational hair care reels, brand storytelling, and customer testimonial highlights. Each piece of content is designed to showcase the salon's expertise in organic, ammonia-free treatments while driving bookings through strategic calls to action.

The Results

A thriving online community that drives real bookings

Through consistent, high-quality content and strategic engagement, the salon has built a loyal following of nearly 2,000 on Facebook, with regular client bookings driven directly from social media content and campaigns.
