The Challenge

Building a social media identity for an automotive lifestyle brand

Rebel Family Motors had a strong offline presence but lacked a cohesive social media strategy. The challenge was creating a content ecosystem that captured the brand's rugged, family-oriented automotive lifestyle while driving engagement and building an online community.

The Solution

Cinematic content meets strategic storytelling

I developed a comprehensive content strategy focused on automotive lifestyle storytelling. This included managing regular posts, stories, and promotional campaigns. I created cinematic video content, designed branded graphics, and implemented an engagement strategy that turned followers into community members.

The Results

A thriving automotive community

The social media strategy transformed Rebel Family Motors from a local business into a recognizable automotive lifestyle brand with a growing, engaged community.
