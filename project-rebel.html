03

Rebel Family Motors

Social Media & Content Strategy

Role Social Media Specialist
Timeline Ongoing
Tools Premiere Pro / Photoshop / Meta
Platform Facebook / Instagram
The Challenge

Cutting through the noise in automotive social media.

Rebel Family Motors needed a social media presence that matched the energy of their brand — bold, unapologetic, and community-driven. The automotive space on social media is saturated, and the challenge was to create content that would stop the scroll, build a loyal following, and convert engagement into actual showroom visits.

The Solution

Cinematic content that builds community.

I developed a content strategy centered on cinematic video production and high-engagement post formats. This included drone shots of inventory, behind-the-scenes team content, customer testimonial reels, and dynamic car reveal videos. Each piece of content was designed for maximum shareability while maintaining a consistent brand voice across platforms.

100+ Assets Created
500k+ Total Reach
2x Engagement Growth
The Result

A brand that lives beyond the lot.

The social media overhaul transformed Rebel Family Motors from a local dealership into a recognized brand in the community. The content strategy established a consistent posting cadence, the video production quality set them apart from competitors, and the engagement metrics proved the ROI of investing in premium content creation.
