The Challenge

Building a premium hair care brand rooted in faith

Deeply Rooted Hair Essentials needed a website that reflected the premium nature of their professional hair botox and scalp treatment products. The challenge was creating a polished e-commerce experience that communicated quality and trust while staying true to their faith-based values — grounded in Jeremiah 17:8.

The Solution

Editorial design meets professional hair care

I designed and developed the full website with warm earthy tones, elegant serif typography, and full-bleed product photography. The site features a product catalog showcasing their Cynty, Michelle, and Scalp Renewal Treatment lines, with direct Shopify integration for seamless purchasing. It also includes a Hair Botox 101 education page, about section, and contact page — all optimized for mobile.

The Results

A brand that looks as premium as it performs

The website elevated DeeplyRooted's brand perception significantly. The editorial-quality design attracted a higher-value customer base, while the optimized checkout flow improved conversion rates.
