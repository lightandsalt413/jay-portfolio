The Challenge

Building a premium brand presence from the ground up

DeeplyRooted needed a website that reflected the premium nature of their hair botox products. The challenge was creating an editorial-quality e-commerce experience that communicated luxury while making the shopping process seamless — all built on Shopify with Mediterranean-inspired aesthetics.

The Solution

Editorial design meets functional e-commerce

I designed and developed a custom Shopify theme with warm earthy tones, elegant serif typography, and full-bleed product photography. The site features a sophisticated product catalog with detailed ingredient pages, a streamlined checkout flow, and responsive design that works flawlessly on mobile devices.

The Results

A brand that looks as premium as it performs

The website elevated DeeplyRooted's brand perception significantly. The editorial-quality design attracted a higher-value customer base, while the optimized checkout flow improved conversion rates.
