Deeply Rooted

Premium Hair Care Brand & E-commerce

Role Creative Developer
Timeline 2 Weeks
Stack HTML / CSS / JS / Shopify
Status ● Live
The Challenge

Building a brand identity from zero.

Deeply Rooted needed more than a website — they needed a complete digital identity. The brand had premium hair care products but no online presence, no e-commerce system, and no visual language to communicate their Mediterranean-inspired philosophy. The challenge was to build everything from scratch while maintaining a luxurious, editorial feel that would match the quality of their products.

The Solution

Mediterranean Boho-Chic meets modern tech.

I designed and developed a full multi-page website with a "Mediterranean Boho-Chic" aesthetic — warm gradients, particle effects, and glassmorphism cards. The site includes a product showcase with direct Shopify integration, a Hair Botox 101 educational page, an About page with founder photography, and a Formspree-powered contact system. Every page was optimized for performance and mobile responsiveness.

6 Pages Built
4 Products Listed
100% Mobile Responsive
The Result

A complete digital storefront.

The final product is a premium, conversion-ready website that positions Deeply Rooted as a professional hair care brand. The Shopify integration enables seamless purchasing, while the editorial design builds instant brand trust. The site was deployed on GitHub Pages with a custom domain configuration.
