The Challenge

Creating a professional digital presence for a trusted Davao City construction firm

FZI Construction, Parts & Supply Co. has been a trusted name in the Davao construction industry for over 20 years. They needed a website that projected professionalism and trust while showcasing their full range of services — general construction, heavy equipment parts, project management, and premium material supply across the Mindanao region.

The Solution

Clean design with strong brand identity

I built a responsive multi-page website from scratch with a professional dark-and-green aesthetic that reflects the brand's identity of growth and reliability. Key features include a project showcase gallery, detailed service breakdowns for construction, equipment, project management and material supply, team profiles, an integrated contact form for quote requests, and a faith-centered brand message.

The Results

Professional credibility that drives business growth

The new website transformed FZI's digital presence, giving them the professional credibility to win larger contracts and attract higher-value clients.
