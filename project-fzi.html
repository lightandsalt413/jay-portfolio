02

FZI Construction

Professional Construction Firm Identity

Role Web Developer / Brand Designer
Timeline 1 Week
Stack HTML / CSS / JS
Status ● Live
The Challenge

Establishing credibility for a growing firm.

FZI Construction had years of experience but no digital footprint. They needed a website that could stand next to industry leaders — one that would communicate professionalism, reliability, and scale. The site needed to showcase their project portfolio, services, and contact information in a way that would inspire trust from potential commercial clients.

The Solution

Editorial light-mode with premium photography.

I designed a high-end, editorial light-mode aesthetic inspired by top-tier construction firms. The site features a professional green accent palette (#2E7D32), a dark navy footer for contrast, optimized project photography grids, and a clean services breakdown. Every element was designed to communicate stability and craftsmanship — from the typography choices to the section spacing.

5 Sections
8 Project Photos
3 Service Areas
The Result

A digital presence that builds trust.

The final website positions FZI Construction as a credible, established firm in the construction industry. The responsive design ensures the site works across all devices, while the project gallery provides social proof of their capabilities. The site is now their primary tool for attracting new commercial contracts.
