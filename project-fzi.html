The Challenge

Creating a professional digital presence for a growing construction firm

FZI Construction needed a website that projected professionalism and trust. The existing online presence was minimal, and the company needed a platform to showcase their portfolio of commercial and residential projects while making it easy for potential clients to request quotes.

The Solution

Clean editorial design with green accent branding

I built a responsive multi-page website from scratch with an editorial light-mode design. The green accent color reinforced the brand's identity of growth and sustainability. Key features included a project showcase gallery, service breakdowns, team profiles, and an integrated contact form with appointment booking capabilities.

The Results

Professional credibility that drives business growth

The new website transformed FZI's digital presence, giving them the professional credibility to win larger contracts and attract higher-value clients.
